Our Mission

Cared4 was founded with a single purpose: to provide thoughtful, tailored care that empowers individuals to live their best lives. We believe in dignity, respect, and the right to quality support, whether at home or in a residential care setting.

Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in supportive living and homemaking services across Florida by providing care that is not only professional but deeply personal.

Our Story

Born out of a passion for service, Cared4 is a licensed provider specializing in developmental disability support. As we expanded, we introduced our Homemaker and Companion Services to broaden our ability to serve individuals in need of home-based assistance, particularly seniors and those recovering from illness or surgery.

Why Choose Us?

  • State-certified, highly trained care professionals
  • Individualized care plans.
  • Consistent, dependable service
  • Deep community roots and a commitment to integrity

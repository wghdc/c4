Getting Started with Cared4 Services

At Cared4, we make the intake process simple, supportive, and transparent. Whether you’re seeking services for a loved one with developmental disabilities or need in-home support for an elderly family member, we’re here to walk with you every step of the way.

Eligibility – Developmental Disability Services

Our developmental disability services are designed for individuals who meet the following criteria:

  • Must be a resident of Florida
  • Have a documented developmental disability (as defined by Florida Medicaid/Waiver guidelines)
  • Be enrolled with or eligible for the Agency for Persons with Disabilities (APD)
  • Require assistance with daily living or support for independent living goals.

Not sure if your loved one qualifies? Contact us and we’ll help you find out.

Eligibility – Homemaker & Companion Services

Our non-medical in-home support is available to:

  • Seniors or adults aged 18+ who require assistance with daily tasks
  • Individuals recently discharged from the hospital or undergoing recovery
  • Families seeking companionship services for a loved one living alone
  • Clients who need help with light housekeeping, errands, or personal support

We currently accept private pay for all homemaker and companion services. Some long-term care insurance policies may reimburse for these services—coverage varies by plan, so we recommend checking with your provider.

Note: As a licensed Homemaker and Companion Services provider through AHCA, we are not authorized to bill Medicaid or Medicare directly.

Our Intake Process

1. Initial Contact

Call, email, or fill out our contact form. A member of our intake team will respond promptly.

2. Consultation & Assessment

We’ll schedule an in-person or virtual consultation to assess needs, eligibility, and preferences.

3. Service Plan Creation

Based on the assessment, we’ll create a customized care plan and schedule services to begin..

4. Start of Care

Your assigned support team will begin services, with ongoing communication and updates.

Developmental Disability Services

APD/Medicaid Waiver Services

We are committed to helping individuals with developmental disabilities lead full, enriched lives through structured support and hands-on care.

Residential Group Home Services

We provide a safe and nurturing living environment where residents receive 24/7 care, personal attention, and daily support in a home-like setting.

Personal Support

One-on-one assistance with daily living tasks including hygiene, grooming, meal prep, and mobility, tailored to the needs and preferences of each individual.

Life Skill Level 1 (Companion)

Non-medical support focused on companionship, community outings, and basic life skills, encouraging social interaction and personal growth.

Respite Care

Short-term care designed to give families and caregivers a much-needed break while ensuring their loved one remains in good hands.

Transportation Assistance

Dependable transport for medical appointments, community engagement, or personal errands, coordinated for convenience and safety.

Services We Provide

Our 24/7 staffed group home offers a full range of supportive services tailored to meet the unique needs of each resident:

  • Personal Supports
    Assistance with daily living activities such as bathing, grooming, dressing, eating, and mobility to promote independence and dignity.
  • Life Skills Level 1 (Companion Services)
    Encouraging socialization, mentoring, and participation in leisure activities while reinforcing basic life and safety skills in a home-like environment.
  • Respite Care
    Short-term care and supervision, giving family members or guardians time to rest and recharge while their loved one remains safe and supported.
  • Transportation Assistance
    Reliable transportation for medical appointments, recreational outings, day programs, and community engagement.
  • 24/7 Staff Supervision
    Compassionate and trained staff are on-site around the clock to ensure safety, consistency, and personalized care.

Our Home

Our welcoming residence is a 4-bedroom, 2-bathroom single-family home designed with both comfort and accessibility in mind.

Home Features Include:

2 Shared Bedrooms (ideal for residents who enjoy companionship)

2 Private Bedrooms (for those who prefer solitude and privacy)

Spacious Family Room for daily interaction, group activities, and relaxation

Modern Kitchen where residents can engage in meal prep and healthy eating habits

Back Patio for outdoor enjoyment and quiet time

Family Visiting Room a designated, private living room area where loved ones can spend quality one-on-one time with their family member

Converted Garage Recreation Room with heating and cooling system perfect for games, crafts, music, and group events

Resident Activities & Community Inclusion

We believe every resident deserves to live a vibrant and fulfilling life. That’s why we focus heavily on community inclusion and resident engagement through a variety of fun and meaningful activities:

• Local community outings (parks, fairs, libraries, festivals)

• Group fitness and walking clubs

• Gardening and outdoor games

• Arts & crafts, music sessions, and movie nights

• Cooking and baking days

• Holiday and birthday celebrations

•Volunteering opportunities and life skill workshops

Our activities are always designed around the interests and goals of our residents, ensuring they stay active, social, and connected to their community.

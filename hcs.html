Homemaker & Companion Services

Non-Medical In-Home Support

(Private Pay)

We provide trusted, compassionate care to adults and seniors who need assistance with daily tasks but wish to remain in their own homes.

Meal Preparation

Nutritious, home-cooked meals prepared with care to meet dietary needs and preferences.

Light Housekeeping

Support with tidying, laundry, and basic household upkeep to maintain a clean, safe living space.

Companionship & Socialization

Engaging conversation, recreational activities, and friendly check-ins to reduce isolation and improve emotional well-being.

Medication Reminders

Helping clients stay on track with medication, never administering, only reminding.

Errands & Appointment Support

Assistance with grocery shopping, pharmacy pickups, and transportation to appointments or events.
